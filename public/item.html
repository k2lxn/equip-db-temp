AC Low Voltage Sensor

Non-contact pickup for verifying the safe absence of 24-90 volts AC in an electrical system.

  • Use as a safety check: red LED indicates AC voltage
  • AC only; unsafe DC voltages will not trigger red LED.
  • Current flow is not necessary to locate voltage.
  • For use on low voltage AC electrical equipment, cables, insulated wires, ...

Technical documentation

Warnings

  • This device detects only AC; it will not detect potentially lethal DC voltages. Not intended for 115V building outlets.
  • When cable such as Romex is wet, this device may respond (wet fiber acts as shield).

Specifications

  • Operating range: 24-90 VAC
  • Operating Temperature: -20 C to +55 C
  • RH/HR 95% (0-30 C); 75 % (30-40 C) 45 % (40-55 C)
  • 3000M: CAT III (UL), CAT IV (TUV) requires 2 AAA batteries

Instructions

  1. Touch the plastic tip to a connection point or move it along an insulated wire; if an AC voltage is present, the LED light in the probe tip will glow red.
  2. The illumination will stop at a break in the circuit or wire.
  3. LED may flicker due to static electricity.
